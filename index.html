Texas Music Documentation Project

Current Project

Austin Music History Project – An online public history project presenting a cultural geography of music performance in Austin. The first exhibit focuses on the period between roughly 1930 and 1950 and is being developed with support from Texas Folklife and a grant from the City of Austin Cultural Arts Division.

About Us

The Texas Music Documentation Project supports the development of public history projects related to music, musicians, and performance in Texas.
